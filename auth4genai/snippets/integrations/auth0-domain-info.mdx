<b>Find your Auth0 domain name</b>

In the Auth0 Dashboard, navigate to `Applications` > `Applications` and select the Auth0 Application you are configuring this connection for. Under the `Settings` tab in the `Basic Information` section you will find your `Domain` name.

If you are using the [custom domains](https://auth0.com/docs/custom-domains) feature, you can find your custom domain in the Auth0 Dashboard by navigating to `Branding` > `Custom Domains`.
</Info>
