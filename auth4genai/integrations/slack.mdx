The Slack Connection enables your GenAI application to authenticate users with their Slack accounts and interact with Slack workspaces on their behalf.

## Overview

The Slack Connection is ideal for AI agents that need to participate in team communications, automate workflows, or provide intelligent assistance within Slack workspaces. This connection provides access to messages, channels, and workspace data.

## Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Slack App">
    1. Go to [Slack API](https://api.slack.com/apps) and create a new app
    2. Configure OAuth & Permissions with required scopes
    3. Note your **Client ID** and **Client Secret**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Auth0 Connection">
    1. In Auth0 Dashboard, go to **Authentication** > **Social**
    2. Select **Slack** and configure with your credentials
    3. Enable **Token Vault** in Advanced settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Required Scopes

| Scope | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `chat:write` | Send messages |
| `channels:read` | View basic information about public channels |
| `users:read` | View people in the workspace |
| `files:write` | Upload and modify files |
