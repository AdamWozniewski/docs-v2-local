## Connect Twitter to Auth0

<Steps>
  <Step title="Twitter setup">
    1. Visit the [Twitter Developer account](https://developer.x.com/) page and sign up.
    2. Follow the onscreen instructions to create a new App in the Twitter Developer Console.
    3. Complete the Consent Screen Information form.
    4. Navigate to Project and Apps and click on your app and go to settings.
    5. Go to User authentication settings and click on edit 
    5. Configure your permissions
    5. Select type of App to enable OAuth2.0 Authentication.
    6. Configure OAuth redirect URL:
       - Redirect URL: `https://YOUR_AUTH0_DOMAIN/login/callback`

To configure the Token Vault for your Twitter connection, you can use the following code snippet in your application:
